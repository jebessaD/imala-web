FAQs

What is the use of Imala?

Imala is designed to minimize problems in transportation. Imala will provide a tarrif of taxi in Addis Ababa as well as Cross country buses from Addis Ababa to other cities in the country. And It will help yoou to report illegal tariff raises

Can I see number of reports on specific car?

Due to legal restrictions only traffic polices can see the number of reports

Is Imala free to use for every one?

Yes! Imala can be used by everyone it is free

How does Imala ensure my privacy?

Your personal informations are not given and used by third parties. We don't use personal data in a way that could cause harm to you and your personal informations are protected.

How Do I Manage My User Account?

You can manage your account in your profile page. You can change your Password, Your Username and You can delete your Account.

Getting started

Checking Taxi tariff

To get the fares of taxi in Addis Ababa city

  1. Go to home page
  2. Select Taxi fares
  3. fill your starting and destination on provided input spaces
  4. click the Search button

Checking Bus tariff

To get the fares of Bus ticket cost from Addis Ababa

  1. Go to home page
  2. Select Bus fares
  3. fill your starting and destination on provided input spaces
  4. click the Search button

Reporting illegals

To report illegal Tariff raises

  1. Go to home page
  2. Login if you are not logged in
  3. Goto Report
  4. Fill the plate number of the car
  5. Fill the road you observed this illegal activity
  6. Write what you observed
  7. Click report

Checking number of Records

To check number of records of specific car you have to login as traffic police

  1. Login as Traffic if you are not logged in
  2. Goto Check Report
  3. Fill the plate number of the car you want to see record
  4. Click Check

Logging in

You have to login to use some features of our website

  1. Go to Login if you have an account or SignUp if you hav enot registered yet
  2. Fill your email and password
  3. click login
